<p>To bring in the new year on a bright note, I decided to turf my old, tired, dark, noisy blue design.  Welcome to <em>paul.annesley.cc &#8212; 2009 edition</em>, with a refreshed, dare I say "cleaner" white design.</p>

<p>Dropping a column and removing all the graphics, backgrounds, borders and so on was a simple task &#8212; a testament to CSS and the separation of structure and presentation.  Adjustments to the navigation and the addition of an <a href="/about">about page</a> were quick thanks to <a href="http://djangoproject.com/">Django</a>.</p>

<p>Enjoy, and happy new year!</p>
